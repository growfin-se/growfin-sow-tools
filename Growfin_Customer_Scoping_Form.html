Cash Application
Automating how you match and post incoming payments
Growfin’s AI (called SmartMatchAI) automatically matches incoming bank payments to the right invoices and posts them to your ERP — eliminating hours of manual work each day.
⏱ About 2 minutes
► See Cash Application in action
ℹHow it works: Your bank sends payment files to a Growfin-managed SFTP folder. Your treasury or bank team needs to initiate this setup with your bank — Growfin provides the SFTP credentials and guides you through it. Our AI (SmartMatchAI) then matches each payment to the right invoices. Your team reviews exceptions and posts — what used to take hours takes minutes.
